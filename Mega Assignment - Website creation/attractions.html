Attractions in Ireland

Cliffs of Moher

The Cliffs of Moher are the Cliffs of Insanity in The Princess Bride. Being such a unique and spectacular site, the cliffs have appeared in various shows and movies in recent years. Most notably, they served as the location for the “Cliffs of Insanity” in the 1987 film The Princess Bride.

Blarney Castle

Blarney Castle is a medieval stronghold in Blarney, near Cork, Ireland. Though earlier fortifications were built on the same spot, the current keep was built by the MacCarthy of Muskerry dynasty, a cadet branch of the Kings of Desmond, and dates from 1446. The Blarney Stone is among the machicolations of the castle.
